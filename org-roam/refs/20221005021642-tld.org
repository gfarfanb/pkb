#+title: ref. TLD
#+filetags: :refs:

In the [[id:531d523f-fc03-4a7b-8b41-e934a76ee275][DNS]] hierarchy, a top-level domain (TLD) represents the first stop after the root zone. In simpler terms, a TLD is everything that follows the final dot of a domain name.
