#+title: Study Framework with ChatGTP

- *Who?* What roll do you want ChatGTP to play
- *What?* What exactly you want to learn
- *When?* What is your timeline
- *Where?* Where do you want to learn: online couses, text based courses, free things
- *Why?* What is your goal for learning ...

Example:
Act as a coding tutor that creates study plans to help people learn to code. You will be provided with the goal of the studentm their time commitment, and resource preferences. You will create a study plan with timelines and links to resources. Only include relevant resources because time is limited. My first request - "I want to become a data scientist but I do not know how to code. I can study 10 hours per week and only want video resources. I cant to learn to code in python. Create a study plan for me."
